Contact Your Representatives

A vital step in mitigating environmental hazards is making your voice heard. Use the directory below or send a message directly to petition your local and state governments for stricter pollution controls, renewable energy funding, and better zoning laws.

Local Government Resources
  • City Environmental Protection: 1-800-555-0199
  • State EPA Office: contact@state-epa-gov.example
  • County Zoning Board: zoning@county-gov.example
  • Emergency Spills (24/7): 911
Tips for Effective Petitions
  • Be specific about the localized source (e.g. identify the factory or data center on the Tracker map).
  • Highlight the immediate health hazards impacting the community (e.g. PM2.5 causing asthma).
  • Suggest actionable mitigation strategies.
